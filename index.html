Privacy Policy for Notes

This app ("Notes", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

1. Data We Access

  • Notes, to-do items, and attachments you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Microphone: used only when you choose to record a voice note.
  • Storage/Files: used to let you attach images, videos, and files to your notes, and to export notes as PDF.
  • Notifications: used to remind you about tasks and reminders you set.

2. Advertising

This app displays ads served by Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), approximate location, and usage data to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can review Google's practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Settings > Google > Ads.

3. Data Storage

Your notes and to-do items are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive database). We do not upload your notes to any external server unless you explicitly choose to export or share them via a third-party app (e.g. email, messaging apps).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google AdMob (for advertising, as described above)
  • Any third-party app you choose to share/export a note to

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Choices

You may delete any note at any time. Deleted notes go to a Recycle Bin and can be permanently removed. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
